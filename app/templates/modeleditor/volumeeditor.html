  The volume parameter is useful when it is necessary to execute a concentration-based model stochastically, or compare the output of a population-based model with concentration data.

  Internally, StochSS converts concentrations to (and from) populations as follows:
  Population = Concentration (moles/cm^3) * Volume (cm^3) * Avagardro's number.
